Why us / Security

Safe Online Shopping

Security is a priority at eXoy and we make every effort to ensure that our transaction process is safe and that your personal information is secure.

  • Do Not Share Personal Information

    eXoy will never e-mail or call you to ask you to disclose or verify your eXoy.in password, credit card or bank-account number, or any other personal information. If you are contacted or receive an unsolicited e-mail which asks you any of this information, disregard the request and report the incident to eXoy.in for investigation.

  • Always Pay Via eXoy Marketplace

    eXoy Marketplace is safe, secure and guaranteed. It provides you with a convenient method of payment and is the only authorised and recognised form of payment on eXoy.in. You should never pay for a Marketplace item outside of the eXoy.in site.

  • Check your Seller's Feedback

    Always check a seller's feedback history before you order an item from them. This is the most important indicator of their overall quality and will give you information about the quality of their products, the speed and quality of their dispatch and delivery and their willingness to resolve transaction disputes.

  • Identify False (Spoof or Phishing) E-mails

    If you receive an e-mail asking you for personal information or directing you to a site other than eXoy.in, or asking you to pay outside of eXoy Marketplace, it could be a "spoof" or "phishing" e-mail and should be considered as fraudulent.
    Genuine eXoy e-mails come from an e-mail address ending in "@exoy.in" or "@exoy.com". If you receive an e-mail in a different format i.e. exoy-security@hotmail.com, you can be sure that it's fraudulent.
    Some phishing e-mails contain links to websites that contain the word "eXoy" somewhere in the URL but will take you to a completely different website. If you hover over the link you often can see the underlying URL which will be in a different format to those linked to within the eXoy sites.
    If you click though on a phishing e-mail and are taken to a page looking like "Your Account" or anything that asks you to verify or change your personal details, you should consider it as fraudulent.

  • Report Phishing E-mails

    E-mail stop-spoofing@exoy.com and attach the e-mail you suspect is fake. By attaching the e-mail we can more easily trace its origins. If you cannot include the e-mail as an attachment, forward it to stop-exoy@amazon.com and include as much of the header information as you can.

  • Choose a Strong Password

    Use a different password on eXoy to what you use on other websites. If you need help remembering your password, write it down and keep it in your purse, wallet, or another secure location. Use at least 8 characters. Though it's not a requirement, adding numbers or special characters to your password will make it even stronger. For example, the password "rivers2!%" is a much stronger password than "rivers." Avoid passwords that are easy to guess, such as "secret," "password," "letmein," "eXoy," or "123456." Avoid using only a dictionary word, your name, e-mail address, or other personal information that can easily be obtained. Passwords are case sensitive. For instance, "RIVERS2!%" and "rivers2!%" are two different passwords. When choosing a password, remember the capitalisation you use, and make sure that the CAPS LOCK key on your keyboard isn't turned on.
